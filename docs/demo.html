Top fixes — ranked by $ impact

Sorted by money on the table, not by how easy they are to spot.

FAIL Conversion tracking is double-counting leads
~$3,100/mo
Rule
R190 No duplicate or double-counted conversions
Evidence
The lead form fires both a GTM tag and a hard-coded gtag on the thank-you page — conversions are inflated ~1.8×, so Smart Bidding is chasing phantom wins.
Fix
Remove the hard-coded gtag; keep the single GTM trigger. Re-baseline tCPA after 2 weeks of clean data.
Effort: Low · Owner: Web/Dev · Fix tracking first — every other number depends on it.
FAIL Best campaign is capped by budget
~$2,400/mo
Rule
R029 Top performers are not limited by budget
Evidence
"Search — Non-Brand Core" hits its daily cap by 2pm and is losing 38% impression share to budget at a CPA 19% under target.
Fix
Raise this campaign's budget 40% and reallocate from "Display — Prospecting" (CPA 3.2× target).
Effort: Low · Owner: Media buyer · Funded winner = fastest ROI win this week.
WARN 19 zero-conversion search terms are eating spend
~$1,800/mo
Rule
R095 Converting-zero search terms above a spend threshold are negated
Evidence
Broad match pulled in 19 terms (incl. "free", "jobs", "diy") with $1,800 spend and 0 conversions in 30 days.
Fix
Add the 19 as exact/phrase negatives; add "free", "jobs", "salary" to the master negative list.
Effort: Low · Owner: Media buyer · Quick win.
WARN Thin ad creative in top-spend ad group
~$1,100/mo
Rule
R122 Headlines cover multiple distinct angles
Evidence
The highest-spend ad group runs one RSA with 4 headlines, all one angle. "Ad strength: Poor."
Fix
Add 11 headlines across 3 new angles (outcome, speed, risk-reversal) and 2 more descriptions.
Effort: Med · Owner: Copywriter (use /ads create).
